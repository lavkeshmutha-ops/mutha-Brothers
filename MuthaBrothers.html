About Our Shop

Established in 1974, Mutha Brothers is a trusted wholesale and retail grocery store serving the Pimpri-Chinchwad community for generations. With decades of experience, we have built a strong reputation based on quality products, fair pricing, and honest business values.

We offer a wide selection of groceries, grains, pulses, oils, and daily essentials at wholesale and retail prices, carefully sourced and sold with integrity. Our commitment to reliable service and customer satisfaction has made Mutha Brothers a name people trust year after year.

At Mutha Brothers, tradition meets quality—ensuring every family gets the best products for their home.
